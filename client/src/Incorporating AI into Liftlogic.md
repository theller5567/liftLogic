Incorporating AI into Liftlogic
separating logic between AI and Liftlogic

The killer feature is making the app feel like a knowledgeable strength coach that can explain every decision. Users are much more likely to trust and stick with a program when they understand why they're doing an exercise, why their weight changed, or why they're not increasing weight yet.

That approach also plays to the work you've already invested in building a structured exercise library, progression rules, onboarding, and workout flow. Instead of replacing that logic with an LLM, AI enhances it by making it conversational, personalized, and transparent.

I think that combination—a deterministic training engine with an AI coach sitting on top—is what could make LiftLogic feel genuinely different from the dozens of apps that simply ask an LLM to write workouts.
